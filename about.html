Our Story

At Hair Day, we believe in more than just a haircut. We craft experiences. Founded by Pria, a visionary colorist with a passion for creativity, and Garry, a skilled hairdresser with an eye for detail, our salon is a sanctuary of transformation. With a staff of seven talented artists, including stylists, colorists, a manicurist, and a makeup artist, every visit promises a personalized touch.

Services

From bold, edgy hues to timeless, classic cuts, we offer a diverse range of services to suit every style. Our small, intimate space ensures that each client receives the utmost attention and care. Due to our high demand for color appointments, we recommend booking in advance to secure your spot in our salon oasis.

Warmth Meets Expertise

Step into a warm, inviting atmosphere designed to make you feel at home. Whether you're seeking a dramatic change or a subtle enhancement, our team listens, advises, and executes with precision. Every visit to Hair Day is an opportunity to rediscover your inner radiance.

Beauty Beyond the Salon

Stay inspired between visits with our exclusive online tutorials. Dive into makeup and nail artistry, guided by our expert team, and unlock the secrets to your own captivating style.

Indulge in luxury, embrace your uniqueness, and let Hair Day be your canvas for expression.
